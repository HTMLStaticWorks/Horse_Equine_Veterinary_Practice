General Inquiries FAQ

Common questions before visiting our clinic.

Can I bring my horse to your clinic instead of a farm call?

Yes, haul-ins are welcome for most procedures and are required for certain surgeries. Please specify "haul-in" when booking your appointment.

What information do you need for a new client?

We need your contact info, farm location, and your horse's basic signalment (age, breed, sex) along with any previous medical records you have.

Experiencing an Emergency?

Do not use the contact form for emergencies. Call our 24/7 hotline directly for immediate assistance.
