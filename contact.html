General Inquiries FAQ

Common questions before visiting our clinic.

Can I bring my horse to your clinic instead of a farm call?
Yes, haul-ins are welcome for most procedures and are required for certain surgeries. Please specify "haul-in" when booking your appointment so our staff is ready to receive you and your horse.
What information do you need for a new client?
We need your contact info, farm location, and your horse's basic signalment (age, breed, sex) along with any previous medical records you have. This ensures our veterinarians are fully prepared before arrival.
Do you offer emergency services for non-clients?
Yes, our 24/7 emergency hotline is available to all horses within our coverage area. However, please be aware that established clients will receive priority dispatch during extreme high-volume situations.
How often should my horse receive dental care?
We strongly recommend a comprehensive dental exam at least once a year for adult horses, and twice a year for seniors or young horses under 5. Routine floating prevents serious digestive and performance issues.

Experiencing an Emergency?

Do not use the contact form for emergencies. Call our 24/7 hotline directly for immediate assistance.
