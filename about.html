About EquineCare

Dedicated to advancing equine health through compassionate care, clinical excellence, and state-of-the-art technology.

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1998, EquineCare started as a single mobile veterinary unit serving local breeders. Today, we have grown into a multi-specialty practice with a fleet of advanced mobile clinics and a central hub for intensive care.

Our commitment has always been to the horse. We continuously invest in the latest diagnostic tools and therapeutic techniques to ensure that our patients receive the highest standard of veterinary care available.
