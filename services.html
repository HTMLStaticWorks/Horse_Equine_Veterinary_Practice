Dental & Reproductive Care

Specialized services for breeding and long-term health.

Routine Floating

Power and hand floating to remove sharp enamel points and balance the dental arcade.

Advanced Extractions

Safe removal of diseased, fractured, or infected teeth using modern surgical techniques.

Breeding Soundness

Comprehensive reproductive evaluations for both mares and stallions before the breeding season.

Artificial Insemination

Expert timing and AI services using fresh, cooled, or frozen semen for optimal conception rates.

Diagnostic Services

Precision technology for accurate diagnosis.

Digital Radiography

High-resolution portable X-rays for immediate stall-side evaluation of bones and joints.

Ultrasonography

Advanced ultrasound for soft tissue injuries, reproductive tracking, and abdominal scans.

In-House Laboratory

Rapid bloodwork, urinalysis, and cytology results processed in our state-of-the-art lab.

Endoscopy

Fiber-optic evaluation of the respiratory and gastrointestinal tracts.
