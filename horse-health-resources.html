Recommended Vaccination Schedules

Core vs. Risk-Based vaccines defined by the AAEP.

Core Vaccines (Annual)

  • Eastern/Western Equine Encephalomyelitis (EEE/WEE)
  • Rabies
  • Tetanus
  • West Nile Virus (WNV)

Risk-Based Vaccines

  • Equine Herpesvirus (Rhino) - Required for competing horses
  • Equine Influenza - Recommended every 6 months for travelers
  • Strangles - Dependent on farm exposure risk
  • Potomac Horse Fever - Based on geographic region
