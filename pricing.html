Pricing FAQ

Common questions about billing and packages.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, all our annual wellness packages can be billed in 12 equal monthly installments via auto-draft at no additional interest charge.

Are medications included in the packages?

Vaccines and routine dewormers specifically listed in the package are included. Prescription medications, joint injections, and emergency drugs are billed separately.

Do you accept equine insurance?

We work with all major equine mortality and major medical insurance providers. We require payment at the time of service but will provide all necessary documentation for you to submit your claim.
