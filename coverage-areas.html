Travel Policies & Availability

Important information regarding farm calls.

Routine Appointments

Please schedule non-emergency farm calls at least 1-2 weeks in advance. Farm call fees are calculated based on mileage from our central clinic. We offer split-call discounts if multiple owners at the same facility schedule on the same day.

Emergency Dispatches

In a life-threatening emergency, our closest mobile unit is dispatched immediately. Emergency farm call fees apply outside of normal business hours (Mon-Fri 8am-5pm) and on holidays.

Out-of-Area Calls

For farm calls outside our standard 100-mile radius, additional travel fees apply. These calls are scheduled based on availability and may require coordination with local veterinary partners.

Cancellation Policy

We kindly request a 24-hour notice for all routine appointment cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a nominal fee to compensate for scheduled travel time.
